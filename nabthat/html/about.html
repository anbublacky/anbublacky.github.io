About Us

Founded in 2012, Nabthat was formed by a diverse team united by a shared experience - namely, the frustration and hassle of buying a new car. We, as a team, refused to take this as a reality and committed ourselves to making a change and bringing “fun” into the car buying experience.

Our goal is to provide an anonymous car buying service that allows you, the car buyer, to shop for a new car wherever, whenever. We have developed a platform that saves you time and money while keeping your identity private so you are not bombarded with unwanted phone calls and emails. We are also the only website that offers you a free credit check, which will guarantee the price of the car and eliminates all unpleasantries that comes with negotiating.
